My Introduction

I’m a results-driven Full-Stack Web & Mobile Developer with 5+ years of experience building high-performance applications that solve real-world problems. My core stack includes React.js, Next.js, TailwindCSS, Bootstrap, TypeScript, Node.js, NestJS, and both SQL (MySQL) and NoSQL (MongoDB) databases.
